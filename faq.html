Before You Book -

How Do I Book An Appointment

You can book an appointment by calling, emailing, or using the “Book Now” button to fill out our online form. Once you have an artist in mind, include details like your idea, size, placement, and any reference images. We’ll review your request and get back to confirm the details.

How Much Does A Tattoo Cost

It depends on the artist, but all tattoos start at $50. The Final price depends on the size, details of the design, the location of placement on the body, and finally which artist you’ve chosen. Some artists have minimum billings, which will vary

How Do I Prepare

Eat beforehand and stay hydrated—low blood sugar can make you feel faint. Avoid alcohol the night before since it increases bleeding and can affect how the tattoo heals. Just relax—you’re in good hands.

Aftercare Basics

Keep your tattoo clean, moisturized, and protected. Gently wash it with mild soap, pat it dry, and apply a thin layer of recommended ointment. Avoid soaking, sun exposure, and picking at scabs while it heals. Follow your artist’s instructions for the best results.

Does It Hurt

Yes! But in the best possible way. Your brain releases endorphins when you go through pain and your body loves endorphins, so yes, it hurts, but you’ll kinda like it.

What Forms Of Payment Are Accepted

We accept cash or credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express). No checks.
