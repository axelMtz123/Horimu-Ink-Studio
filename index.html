EXPERIENCE

The studio is built around a calm, focused environment where clients can feel comfortable from start to finish. We prioritize clear communication, respect for first-timers and collectors alike, and a process that removes uncertainty at every step. From consultation to final session, everything is handled with care and intention.

PROCESS

Consultation – We discuss your idea, meaning, placement, and style direction

Design – A custom piece is created specifically for your body and concept.

Approval – You review the design and we refine it if needed.

Session – The tattoo is executed with focus on precision and comfort.

Aftercare – Clear guidance is given to make sure the piece heals properly and stays clean long-term.
