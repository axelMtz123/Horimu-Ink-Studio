About Isaiah

Specializes in:

Black & Gray

Other Styles:

  • Anime
  • Neo-Traditional

Isaiah specializes in Black & Grey, Anime, and Neo-Traditional tattooing, creating bold and dynamic designs with strong structure and visual impact.

His work blends clean linework, contrast, and composition, bringing both anime characters and neo-traditional concepts to life with clarity and personality.

With a focus on balance between detail and overall flow, his tattoos are designed to remain visually strong and readable over time.
