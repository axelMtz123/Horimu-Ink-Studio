About Michael

Specializes in:

Thai Hand-poked / Sak Yant

Micheal specializes in Thai hand-poked tattooing (Sak Yant), focusing on traditional techniques and cultural authenticity.

Unlike machine tattooing, each piece is created by hand, resulting in a unique texture, rhythm, and organic flow.

His work respects the meaning and structure behind each symbol, offering tattoos that carry not only visual impact, but also cultural and spiritual significance.
