Specializes in:

Fine Art

Other Styles:

Graduated from an art academy with a strong foundation in drawing and visual aesthetics, Li demonstrates exceptional precision in fine line and black & grey work, while also excelling in color design and application.

Her work is known for its clean, delicate execution, with color used thoughtfully to enhance the design without overwhelming it.
